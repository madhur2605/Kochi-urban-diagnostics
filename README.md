***Index Map Suite***

Ward-level spatial index maps developed to inform decision-making of Kochi Municipal Corporation (KMC).
Each map visualises a distinct dimension of urban health infrastructure and service accessibility across Kochi's wards.

## 🏛️ Context

This work was completed as part of the **Urban Immersion Program (2nd year)** under TISS Mumbai at Kochi (Kerala), in partnership with **Kochi Municipal Corporation (KMC)**, **C-HEAD**, and **GIZ**.
Findings, Case Studies, and dashboards were presented to KMC and GIZ officials.

> *Part of broader fieldwork under the BS Analytics & Sustainability Studies programme, TISS Mumbai.*
